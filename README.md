# Brihat Sharma

ML engineer in Chicago. I work on LLM evaluation methodology and clinical NLP, and I build things on the side.

## Research

I publish in clinical NLP and AI fairness. Full list: [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=gWK5o2EAAAAJ&hl=en).

First-author work:

- Multi-Task Training with In-Domain Language Models for Diagnostic Reasoning. *5th Clinical NLP Workshop, ACL 2023.*
- Bias and fairness assessment of a natural-language-processing opioid-misuse classifier: detection and mitigation across racial subgroups. *JAMIA, 2021.*
- Publicly available machine-learning models for identifying opioid misuse from clinical notes of hospitalized patients. *BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making, 2020.*

Selected co-authored work:

- Transatlantic transferability and replicability of machine-learning algorithms to predict mental-health crisis. *Nature Digital Medicine, 2024.*
- DR.BENCH: Diagnostic Reasoning Benchmark for Clinical Natural Language Processing. *Journal of Biomedical Informatics, 2023.*
- Development and multimodal validation of a substance-misuse algorithm for referral to treatment using AI (SMART-AI). *Lancet Digital Health, 2022.*
